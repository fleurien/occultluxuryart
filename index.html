Welcome

You can call me Adrien, Adri, or Pitou; I'm not that picky! I'm interested in things like the psych horror/thriller genre as a whole, baking shows, and open world rpgs.

Most of my characters are involved in an ongoing story and world with my boyfriend, but I'm always happy to talk about cs links and interactions. I currently only have time to roleplay with close friends, but I'm open to headcanon or talk about ocs with anyone over 18.
